AI Video Works


barefoot labyrinth (soft relax media, 2025) - trailer | full movie - with this feature, I used a variety of AI video tools including QwenAI, Hunyuan, and Wan2.2. I also prompted DeepSeek to create the monologues, and used Edge TTS for text-to-speech voice generation. My intention was to create a body horror sci-fi documentary that focused primarily on female feet in a fetishistic context, while subverting audience expectations. Note: mildly not safe for work, though the work exists in an artistic context.

breath of knees (soft relax media, 2025) - trailer | full movie - with this feature, I used Hunyuan, Hailou, and Qwen to create a multi-faceted narrative. I used Yuewen for all animal and cartoon-like imagery. My intention was to create a found footage documentary that floated between dream-logic and reality and acted as a commentary on belief, life, and death.


torment of the witch (cinema epoch, 2025) - trailer - I used DeepSeek to write code for SuperCollider, an audio synthesis and generation application. I then further produced the output to match my intended aesthetic.

infat***ed dot ai - nsfw ai companion site - I created 90% of the characters here, writing their bios, crafting their appearance, and building their image ensuring their configurations were unique and on-brand, as well as generating their images and their welcome videos and avatar videos. I have also tested various parts of the site's "Create" tool that allows users to create their own AI companions.
